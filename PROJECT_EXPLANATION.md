# 🌍 Project Explanation – Uganda Climate Guard 2026

## 🇺🇬 Problem in Uganda

Uganda faces recurring climate-related disasters, especially during the March–May (MAM) rainy season. These include:

* **Landslides** in highland areas like Bududa and Sironko due to steep terrain and prolonged rainfall.
* **Flooding** in low-lying and urban areas such as Kampala (Bwaise), caused by poor drainage and heavy storms.
* **Drought** in the cattle corridor (e.g., Moroto, Nakasongola), affecting livestock and water availability.

The main challenge is not just the weather itself, but **lack of accessible, localized, and actionable information**. Many communities receive general forecasts but **do not get specific guidance on what actions to take**, which increases risk and loss.

---

## 💡 Why This App Matters

Uganda Climate Guard 2026 is designed to bridge the gap between **weather data and real-life decision-making**.

This app:

* Provides **district-specific forecasts**, not generic national reports
* Translates weather conditions into **clear risk levels (🟢 🟡 🔴)**
* Gives **life-saving emergency protocols** for floods and landslides
* Supports farmers with **seasonal planting advice**
* Uses **local context and familiar references** (e.g., Bwaise flooding, MAM rains)

Instead of just telling users *what the weather is*, the app tells them:
👉 **What it means**
👉 **Why it matters**
👉 **What they should do next**

This makes it a **decision-support tool**, not just a weather app.

---

## 📊 How the Risk Score Works

The app uses a transparent **Risk Score system (0–100%)** to quantify danger levels.

The score is calculated based on multiple real-world factors:

* **+40%** → If the district is in a known high-risk zone (landslide, flood, or drought)
* **+25%** → If rainfall is torrential or above normal
* **+15%** → If it is during April (peak rainfall period in Uganda)
* **+10%** → If the ecological zone increases risk (e.g., Elgon Highlands, Lake Victoria Basin)
* **+10%** → If soil saturation (“sponge effect”) is present

### 🎯 Interpretation:

* **0–30% → 🟢 STABLE** (low risk)
* **31–60% → 🟡 CAUTION** (moderate risk)
* **61–100% → 🔴 DANGER** (high risk, immediate action required)

This system ensures:

* The AI is **consistent**
* The output is **explainable**
* Users can **understand the level of risk clearly**
